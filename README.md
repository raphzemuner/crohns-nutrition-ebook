# Building Muscle with Crohn's Disease
### A Complete Nutrition & Lifestyle Guide

A free, self-contained HTML eBook for people living with Crohn's disease who want to build muscle, manage flares, and take control of their health through diet and lifestyle.

---

## What's Inside

**13 chapters covering:**

1. Understanding Crohn's & Nutrition
2. Caloric & Protein Strategy
3. Safe Foods & What to Avoid
4. Supplement Stack for Crohn's
5. Sample Meal Plans
6. Training Around Crohn's
7. Your First Year — What to Expect
8. Foods That Heal the Gut
9. Medication-Free Approaches
10. All Dietary Routes (Carnivore, SCD, Mediterranean, Low-FODMAP, Paleo, Vegetarian, Vegan, EEN & more)
11. Mind-Gut Connection — Stress & Exercise
12. Real Cases & Clinical Studies
13. Interactive Plans — Remission, Flare & Vegan tabs

---

## How to Use

Just open `nutrition-crohns-muscle.html` in any browser — no internet connection, no installation, no dependencies.

Works on desktop and mobile (iPhone/Android optimized).

---

## Features

- Fully offline — single HTML file, no external resources
- Mobile-friendly with iPhone notch support
- Interactive tab section for In Remission / During a Flare / Vegan plans
- Scrollable sticky navigation
- Printable

---

## Disclaimer

This guide is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult your gastroenterologist or a registered dietitian before making significant changes to your diet, especially during active disease.
